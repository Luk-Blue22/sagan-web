02
How screen captures work

SAGAN automatically captures your screen at intervals of 3 to 30 seconds (configurable based on your plan) while the application is active. These captures are stored exclusively in your computer's RAM — they are never written to the hard drive.

Important: Captures are not sent anywhere automatically. They are only transmitted to the AI model at the exact moment you make a query, and only the 3 most recent captures (configurable).

Once SAGAN responds to your query, the captures that were part of that query are discarded from the buffer. When closing SAGAN, the entire buffer is securely wiped — bytes are overwritten with zeros before being released.

Automatically excluded apps: SAGAN detects the active Windows window before each capture. If the app matches our blacklist (40+ sensitive apps: banks, password managers, authenticator apps, crypto wallets), the capture simply is not taken. You can add any additional app in Settings → Privacy → Blacklist.

What we cannot guarantee: If you have a password visible on screen outside a blacklisted app (for example, in a notepad), SAGAN could capture it. Our sensitive pattern filter will try to detect it before sending, but it is not infallible. Activate private mode (Win+Shift+P) when handling very sensitive information.
03
AI Engine — what it receives and how it handles it

SAGAN uses state-of-the-art AI engines to process your queries. When you ask a question, the engine receives your text and the necessary screen captures as visual context.

What the AI engine receives: Your question in text + up to 3 screen captures from the moment of the query.

What it does NOT receive: Your full history, your audio, your files, or captures outside the moment of the query.
Recommendation: If you handle confidential client information, contracts or medical data, we recommend using Private Mode during those sessions (Win+Shift+P), or considering whether SAGAN is the right tool for that specific context.

We do not store the captures that are processed. Once the query ends, the images are not kept on our servers.

04
Voice and audio — local processing

Voice processing in SAGAN is 100% local. Both Speech-to-Text (STT) and Text-to-Speech (TTS) run directly on your computer using locally installed models:

STT (voice recognition): High-precision local engine — runs on your CPU/GPU without sending audio to servers.

TTS (voice synthesis): Natural multilingual voice engine — runs completely on your device.

Your audio never leaves your computer. Not even for transcription.

The only thing that travels to the AI model is the transcribed text of your question — never the original audio.

05
Account and payment data

We store in Supabase (encrypted database) only what is necessary to manage your account: name, email, active plan, usage counters and registration date. Your password is stored as a bcrypt hash — never in plain text.

Payments are processed exclusively by Stripe. We never see, store or have access to your credit card data. Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 Compliant.

Our database has Row Level Security (RLS) enabled: each user can only access their own data. There is no administrative view that allows our employees to read your query history.

08
Technical security

We implement the following technical measures to protect your data:

Encryption in transit: HTTPS/TLS 1.3 on all communications between the app and our servers.

Encryption at rest: API keys stored with AES-256. Passwords hashed with bcrypt.

RAM-only for captures: Images never touch the hard drive. Secure wipe on close.

Sensitive data scan: Pattern scanning (cards, tokens, ID numbers) before sending to LLM.

Row Level Security: Data isolation at database level in Supabase.

If you find a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to seguridad@sagan-app.com. We appreciate responsible disclosure.
